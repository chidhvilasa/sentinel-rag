# Company Policy Document
## Employee Handbook - Section 7: Remote Work

### 7.1 Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their probationary period are eligible for remote work arrangements.

### 7.2 Equipment
The company provides standard equipment including:
- Laptop computer
- External monitor
- Keyboard and mouse

### 7.3 Work Hours
Remote employees must maintain standard business hours (9 AM - 5 PM local time) unless otherwise approved.
